    Plot Summary

    In a far away kingdom Cinderalla lived happily with her mother and father until her mother passed away.Cinderalla's father soon remarries a cruel woman. The woman had two daughters,Drizella and Anastasia. When the father passed,Cinderalla's wicked stepmother turns her into a virtual servant.

    Theme

    Cinderella teaches the morals of kindness towards everyone. She forgives others for doing her wrong. She doesn't let anyone ruin her good heart. The theme of the story are good versus evil.
